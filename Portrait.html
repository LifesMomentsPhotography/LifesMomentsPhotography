Portrait Photography

What is Portrait Photography?

Portrait photography captures the essence of humans, whether in a studio or outdoor setting, serving various purposes from professional profiles to personal decor, aiming to capture beautiful moments and celebrate individuality.

Pricing Rates

Headshots - $50/hr

Solo headshots, taken from the hips up, waist up, or shoulders up upon request. Best for Profressional profile photos to be used for resumés, websites, advertisemnts, and more. Personality shots will also be included.

Family Portraits - $70/hr

Group photos of the entire family. Some candid portrait shots as well as personality shots if desired. Capture beautiful memories with the ones closest to your heart!

Solo Portraits - $55/hr

Graduation photos or just because photos! Don't miss an opportunity to capture your inner and outer beauty or achievemnts. Professional, fun or both! Book a session and live in the moment. Props are welcomed and can be provided based on the occasion.

Information and Tips for your Photo Session

Prepare for your photography session by communicating your expectations and dressing in what suits you, ensuring your clothing complements the setting and you. Relax, be yourself, and trust your photographer's guidance while staying hydrated and well-rested for optimal results. Bring props if desired, stay open to suggestions, and above all, enjoy the experience—it's about capturing beautiful moments you'll cherish.
