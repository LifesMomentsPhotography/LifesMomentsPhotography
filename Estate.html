Estate Photography

What is Estate Photography?

We turn bricks and mortar into pixel-perfect dreams. We capture the essence of your property, from cozy corners to grand facades, in snapshots that make houses feel like homes before you even step inside!

Pricing Rates

The Ins & Outs - $120/hr

This session we capture the inner and outter beauty of your home. We Capture the cozy essence that makes a house a home. We will bring some housing props to use if needed to capture the perfect shot of your estate. Just sit back and relax as you watch us make your estate into the perfect forever home and paradise.

Everywhere and in the Air - $180/hr

This session includes everything from the other package but with bonus aerial shots of the home to add just the right detail to your estate listings. With the use of drones and our professional cameras we capture the magnificence of your home from every angle!

Information and Tips for your Photo Session

Prepare your estate for its close-up by decluttering rooms, staging furniture to showcase space, and adding pops of color with fresh flowers or accent pieces. Ensure all lights are working and windows are clean to maximize natural light, and don't forget to tidy up outdoor areas for picture-perfect curb appeal!
